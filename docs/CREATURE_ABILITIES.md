## Stat Conversion Guide

- **Mythrill STR/AGI/CON/INT/SPI/CHA** ranges from 4 (terrible) to 20+ (godlike)
- **HP ranges**: Low (30-60), Medium (60-120), High (120-250)
- **Mana ranges**: Low (0-20), Medium (20-60), High (60-100)
- **AP ranges**: Low (3), Medium (3-4), High (4-5)
- **Speed ranges**: Low (10-30), Medium (25-45), High (35-60)
- **Resistances**: 25% (weak), 50% (moderate), 75% (strong), 100% (immune)
- **Damage notation**: XdY+Z (e.g., 2d8+4 = two 8-sided dice + 4 modifier)
- **DC saves**: DC 12-18, usually vs AGI, CON, or SPI

## Campaign Integration

- **Low danger**: 1-3 creatures, designed for first encounters
- **Medium danger**: 3-6 creatures, designed for mid-game challenges
- **High danger**: 6-10 creatures, designed for boss encounters
- **Legendary**: 10+ creatures, designed for climactic battles
